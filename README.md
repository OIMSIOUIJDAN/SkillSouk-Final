## 🌟 Overview
**SkillSouk** reimagines the traditional Moroccan marketplace (Souk) as a digital learning ecosystem. Instead of trading goods, students trade knowledge. By combining deep gamification, immersive environments, and AI-powered learning tools, SkillSouk transforms studying from a stressful chore into an engaging, collaborative RPG.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
SkillSouk solves three critical campus issues:
1. **Academic Intimidation:** Students are often too shy to ask questions in person, fearing judgment. The virtual Souk removes this social anxiety.
2. **Toxic Competition:** Campus culture often feels like a zero-sum game. SkillSouk shifts this to collaborative growth through guilds and shared goals.
3. **"Fake Studying":** Students fall into the trap of studying for aesthetics without deep focus. Our immersive Focus Zones and RPG Boss Battles ensure actual, productive work.

## ✨ Core Features

### 🎮 The Virtual Souk (Knowledge Marketplace)
A judgment-free zone where students trade study materials. Users can act as **"Seekers"** (buying help and materials) or **"Merchants"** (selling knowledge and earning coins).

### 🌌 Immersive Focus Zones
Atmospheric, dreamy environments designed for deep work:
- **Library Zone:** Silent study with full concentration.
- **Study Café:** Ambient noise for relaxed learning.
- **Night Focus:** Deep focus mode for late-night exam prep.

###  Progression & Economy
- **Souk Coins:** Earned by uploading materials, helping peers, or completing focus sessions.
- **XP & Levels:** Accumulate "Focus Hours" to level up and unlock new Academic Districts (Computer Science, Medicine, Arts, etc.).
- **Streaks & Badges:** Daily streaks reward consistency. Badges like "Focus Master" and "Top Explainer" recognize deep work.

## 🚀 Advanced Gamification

### 🐾 Souk Companion (Virtual Pet)
An emotional companion that evolves through 3 stages (🥚 Souk Egg → 🦊 Desert Fox → 🦅 Souk Eagle) based on user level. It features a happiness meter that increases when you complete focus sessions.

###  Focus Battles (RPG Boss Fights)
Turns the Pomodoro timer into an epic RPG battle. Students fight monsters like the **"Procrastination Slime"** or the **"Theorem Titan"**. Every minute of focus deals damage to the boss. If the user switches tabs, the monster attacks back!

### ⚔️ Scholar Duels (PvP)
Challenge peers to quick quiz battles. Winners steal coins from the loser and earn prestige, turning knowledge review into a highly competitive game.

### 🏕️ Knowledge Caravans (Guilds)
Students form guilds of 3-5 members with shared weekly study goals. If the caravan hits its target, everyone gets massive XP and coin bonuses.

### 🏮 Mystery Lanterns (Loot Boxes)
Level-up rewards that explode with particle effects, revealing random cosmetic items (Golden Turban, Sapphire Sash) or massive coin bonuses.

### 🎪 Live Events
Time-limited festivals (e.g., "The Midnight Exam Rush") with countdown timers and 2x XP perks to drive daily engagement.

## 🤖 AI Integration

### ✨ AI Study Forge
A game-wide AI tool where students can upload PDFs/documents. The AI instantly generates a concise summary and a graded multiple-choice quiz, moving users from passive reading to active recall.

### 🏆 Class Rank Leaderboard
A competitive leaderboard that ranks all users like a real classroom based strictly on their "Average Quiz Grade" from the AI Study Forge. Students earn academic titles:
- **Grandmaster** (90-100%)
- **Scholar** (80-89%)
- **Apprentice** (60-79%)
- **Novice** (<60%)
